Reforge Product Strategy course

The Product Strategy course from Reforge was a 6-week intense virtual program, which I completed in 2020, that included more than 12 hours of presentations / content as well as weekly live case studies and Q&A by industry experts.

I was able to apply several of the concepts I learned at Design Pickle as I led the Product Team, helped set and focus the team short-term and long-term strategies, leveled the team up through training and hiring, and continually evolved the platform. I am passionate about user experience and how a great product can result in loyal customers, and I love digging into growth and expansion strategies. Although my true passion is engineering, I believe that having a higher-level persepective of how everything works together and always thinking strategically is critical at all levels of a company.
